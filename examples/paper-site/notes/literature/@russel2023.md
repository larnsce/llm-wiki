# Russel et al. 2023 — Longitudinal adoption in three CBS programmes (example entry)

Fixture literature note.

## Summary

- Follows subscriber cohorts in three [[wiki/concept/container-based-sanitation|CBS]] programmes over 30 months.
- Reports monthly churn of 2–4% with **re-subscription in 31% of churned households** (cohort B).
- Collection reliability predicts retention better than price in all three programmes.

## Relevance to the paper

The re-subscription finding grounds the hub's draft decision of 2026-06-19:
adoption is modelled as a panel with re-entry, churn is not an absorbing
state.
