# Sanitation service chain

The sequence of services that safely manage excreta from capture to reuse
or disposal: capture → containment → emptying → transport → treatment →
reuse/disposal. A system is only as safe as its weakest link.

## Key claims

- Full-chain evaluation is the appropriate lens for comparing sanitation service models in dense settlements. — cite: [[notes/literature/@tilley2024|tilley2024]]
